Laying || Set up

For installation of decorative stone and bricks offer to apply adhesives, cementitious grout, and other supplies, according to the instructions for use. Adhesive is recommended for assembly works at a surface temperature not lower than +5 C. For example, the adhesive composition Knauf Flesenkleber "N" for the interior or exterior Knauf Flesenkleber.

Preparing for the installation of artificial stone

Before you begin, be sure to mix the artificial stone of not less than 5 (preferably 10 boxes) and lay on the ground next drawing laying. This will facilitate the solution with a choice of separate elements of stones (size and color) in the process. To fit the stone size, use angle grinders machine ( "Bulgarian") with a disk of stone. To the back of the artificial stone was missing "cement milk" (a thin foam layer 0.5 mm thick) need to clean the back of the stone stiff wire brush. 15-20 min before laying or to humidify the diluted 1:10 with water acrylic primer with a paint brush surface on which is mounted an artificial stone. Subsequently, on the surface and working side-stone causing the adhesive. Laying of artificial stone is best done from the top down, from the first tile in any corner where according to calculations should be the first uncircumcised tiles. This will avoid getting glue on the already stacked stone rows. If you purchased the angular elements, artificial stone installation, start with their installation, alternately alternating short and long sides of the corners. Work is carried out, as well as, in general, from the top down. Installation of decorative stone with seams possible without them. In the first case, the adhesive must be applied more. This will give the opportunity to immediately "rozshyt" joints as excess glue, pressing himself form the seam and a beautiful texture that resembles the old masonry. When laying an artificial stone with jointing always keep in mind and follow the width of the gap between the stones. Decorative stone "Verona" is placed with the seam h = 0,5 mm for the "Austrian" brick h = 1-1,5 mm and for the "Modena» - h = 0 mm (seamless). When installing the product "Austrian" bricks, we recommend to add to the composition pecking dyes (zhovto, Chervoniy and so on. D.) Of your choice, which will allow to further enhance the properties of decorative brickwork.

Laying decorative stone

Using a trowel, apply on the back side facing the adhesive layer elements for laying a thickness of 0.5 to 1 cm. Make sure that the solution is required to cover the entire surface. You squeeze element facing the wall. For best fixation of slightly rotate it. When this solution has slightly squeezed out from all sides of the set element. With seamless masonry should pay particular attention to the fact that end-jointed cladding elements are filled a thin layer of solution.

    To do this:
  • solution applied completely covering the reverse side of the covering element, so that when installing the excess squeezed out at the edges;
  • immediately after installation, using the trowel, remove any excess solution, leaving it thin layer at the ends.
If cladding installed on a very dry surface or assembly is carried out in conditions of hot and dry weather, the elements installed and the surface is recommended to moisten with a sprayer or paint brush. Sticker are small patches (0.3-0.6 sq m) in the direction from top to bottom - so sinters adhesive solution is less stain decorative surface.

Grouting for laying of artificial stone

Fancy - important stage of laying artificial stone. When it is necessary to conduct a thorough achieve sealing surface, eliminating the ingress of moisture under the liner. This avoids cracking of the lining elements in the winter and as a result, destruction of masonry. Fancy spend building spraying pistol or using a special bag for grouting solution. This bag is easy to do yourself. It is sufficient to cut one of the corners of the medium size conventional plastic package and to make it such that the resulting exit aperture diameter was a little smaller than the width of joints between lining elements. Fill the bag with a solution. Slowly squeezing out a solution through a hole in the bag, gently fill them joints. Be careful that the solution did not get to the liner. After the solution will seize, pointing trowel to smooth out dents movements joints and remove excess solution. Do not try to handle the seams immediately after their completion, as has not yet been setted solution is left on the wall dirty footprints. Stiff brush (! Not metal) finally smooth out the surface after 40 - 60 minutes. Decorative stones do not need special care. The interiors clean the surface of the ordinary detergent enough and exteriors - high pressure water.

A living room is often the coziest space in the house where you can gather with your family and friends, and every designer or just owner tries to make it as inviting as possible. A fireplace, some plants and fur would make your living room cozy, and of course, a stone or brick wall! Yes, it’s difficult to believe it but rough brick makes any space cozy and joyful, despite of the style you’ve chosen. Actually, one brick wall is easy to adapt to your interior and colors – just choose the size and the colors you need – white for shabby chic or strict dark grey for an industrial space. Very often a fireplace is done in brick and the wall behind it continues the décor.

A lot of people like the look of exposed brick walls. They became popular in late 1900s because many people found that it was far less expensive to expose the brick in older homes then it was to repair or replace the damaged plasterwork. They add character and rustic charm to any interior and can make any room looks quite industrial. Especially popular they are in modern lofts and in historical buildings but noways you can find exposed bricks in other living spaces too.
